Training data rarely lives in one place. It is scattered across tables, files, and silos, each
with its own schema and quirks. **Amalur** asks a simple question: what if data integration and
machine learning were designed together, rather than bolted on after the fact?

By treating integration as part of the learning pipeline, Amalur automates much of the tedious work
of bringing scattered sources into a form that models can actually use — the first step toward our
broader Model Lake vision.
