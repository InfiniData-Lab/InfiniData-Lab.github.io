Rihan Hai is an Assistant Professor at TU Delft, where she leads the Infinidata Lab. She
received her PhD with highest honours from RWTH Aachen University and is an NWO VENI laureate.
Her research spans data lakes and data integration, machine-learning systems, and quantum data
management.
