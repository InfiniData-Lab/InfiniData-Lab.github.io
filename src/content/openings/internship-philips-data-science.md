An **internship with Philips** applying generative AI to unstructured maintenance text — **extracting
structured information**, classifying documents, and improving information retrieval.

You will evaluate state-of-the-art NLP methods, prototype with real service reports and technician
notes, collaborate with domain experts, and present your findings to stakeholders.

**Good fit if** you are pursuing a BSc, MSc, or PhD in CS, AI, or Data Science, with a good grasp of ML
and generative AI and proficiency in Python; familiarity with PyTorch, TensorFlow, or Hugging Face
Transformers is a plus.
